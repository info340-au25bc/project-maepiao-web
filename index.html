HomeVest

Real Estate ROI Simplified

Problem

Buying a home is one of the biggest financial decisions people make, yet most online tools only show the price tag—not the full picture. Platforms like Zillow and Redfin are great for browsing homes, but they don’t help users understand how much they can actually afford, or whether a home could be a good investment. For example, a listing might show an estimated $3,000 monthly mortgage, but doesn’t account for property taxes, potential rental income, or appreciation. For many young buyers and aspiring investors, that makes it hard to make informed choices

Our project, Homevest, focuses on solving that problem. It allows users to plug in a few numbers—like price, rent, loan-term, credit score, or down payment—and instantly see how much profit or loss they might make over time. Unlike Zillow’s simple mortgage calculator, our app provides a dynamic, interactive interface where users can adjust financial assumptions and visualize long-term results. We want to make real estate finance more transparent and accessible, even for users who aren’t experts
